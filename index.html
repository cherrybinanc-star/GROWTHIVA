FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What services does Growthiva offer?

We offer a comprehensive suite of digital services including website design & development, e-commerce solutions (Shopify, WooCommerce), branding & identity, digital marketing, SEO, social media marketing, mobile app development, and video production. From strategy to execution, we've got you covered.

How much does a website cost?

The cost of website design varies depending on the size and complexity of the project. A simple landing page is vastly different from a full e-commerce store. Get in touch with us for a free consultation and custom quote tailored to your specific needs.

What makes Growthiva different from other agencies?

Three things: 100% custom designs (no templates), transparent pricing (no hidden fees), and proactive client service (we chase you, not the other way around). Plus, our five-day sprint methodology means your project moves at lightning speed.

How long does it take to build a website?

A typical website takes 2-6 weeks depending on complexity. Simple landing pages can be done in a week. Full e-commerce stores or web applications may take 4-8 weeks. We work in five-day sprints, so you see progress fast.

Do you provide ongoing support after launch?

Absolutely! We offer ongoing maintenance, updates, and support packages. Think of us as your digital emergency pharmacy — whenever you need pixel fixes, content updates, or strategic adjustments, we're just a call away.

Can you help improve our search engine rankings?

Yes! Website design has a significant impact on SEO. We build for performance — fast loading, mobile-responsive, well-organized, and easy to navigate — all critical factors that search engines love. Check out our SEO packages for dedicated SEO services.
